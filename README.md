# Data Science Salary Dashboard

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze complaint patterns across time, geography, issue types, response status, and submission channels using an interactive dashboard with key KPI tracking and visual exploration.

## Dataset Used
- <a href="">HR Dataset</a>

## Visualization Overview
- **Company Size Across Countries** shows the proportion of different company sizes across countries.
- **Job Experience Levels Across Countries** shows the proportion of job positions at different experience levels across countries.
- **Employment Types Across Countries** shows the proportion of different employment types across countries.
- Top 10 Employee Countries of Residence shows the top 10 countries where employees are located.
- Average Salary (USD) by Experience Level and Employment Type shows the average salary for each combination of experience level and employment type, with a job title filter to select different roles.
- Average Salary by Country shows average salary by country on a map, where color intensity represents salary levels, with a job title filter to select different roles.
- Average Salary by Job Title and Experience Level displays a pivot table of average salary with job titles as rows and experience levels as columns, and a company location filter to control countries.
